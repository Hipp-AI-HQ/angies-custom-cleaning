## Services

### Office Cleaning
Weekly or nightly commercial cleaning tailored to your schedule. We keep your workplace spotless so you can focus on your business. Our background-checked team handles everything from dusting and vacuuming to sanitizing surfaces and restocking supplies.

### Carpet Steam Cleaning
Deep steam cleaning that removes dirt, stains, and allergens from your office carpets. We use professional-grade equipment to restore your carpets to like-new condition, extending their life and improving indoor air quality.

### Floor Buffing
Professional floor buffing and polishing services that restore the shine to your commercial floors. Regular buffing protects your investment and creates a professional appearance that impresses clients and customers.

### Floor Stripping & Waxing
Complete floor stripping and waxing service that removes old buildup and applies fresh protective coating. This intensive process revitalizes worn floors and provides long-lasting protection against daily wear and tear.

### Floor Polishing
Regular floor polishing maintenance keeps your commercial floors gleaming between major services. Our polishing service maintains the professional appearance your business deserves while protecting your flooring investment.

## About

### Angie's Custom Cleaning Story

Founded in 2012, Angie's Custom Cleaning was created to address a critical need in Lancaster County: high-quality commercial cleaning services delivered with integrity, reliability, and attention to detail.

What started as a one-woman operation has grown into a trusted team of 20 background-checked, insured professionals. This growth reflects our unwavering commitment to superior service at ultra-competitive prices—a combination that has earned us awards, loyal clients, and the reputation as Lancaster's favorite commercial cleaning service.

We understand that your office is more than just a workplace—it's where you build your business, serve your clients, and spend a significant portion of your day. That's why we don't just clean; we maintain your clean legacy with the same care and professionalism you bring to your own work.

Every member of our team is thoroughly background-checked, bonded, and insured. We conduct frequent quality inspections to ensure our high standards are maintained across every job site. Whether you need weekly office cleaning, carpet steam cleaning, or floor maintenance, you can trust Angie's Custom Cleaning to deliver exceptional results.
