## SERVICES SECTION

**Section Title:** Our Cleaning Services

**Section Intro:** Whether you need weekly maintenance or a one-time deep clean, we customize our services to fit your home and schedule.

### Service 1: Regular Cleaning

**Description:**
Weekly or bi-weekly home cleaning that keeps your living spaces fresh and welcoming. We handle dusting, vacuuming, mopping, bathroom sanitizing, and kitchen cleaning so you can spend time on what matters most.

**What's Included:**
- Dusting all surfaces, furniture, and shelves
- Vacuuming carpets and rugs
- Mopping hard floors
- Bathroom sanitizing (toilets, sinks, tubs, showers)
- Kitchen surface cleaning and appliance wiping
- Trash removal

**Best For:** Occupied homes, weekly or bi-weekly maintenance, busy families

### Service 2: Deep Cleaning

**Description:**
Thorough top-to-bottom cleaning for occupied homes. We reach the areas often overlooked—baseboards, ceiling fans, behind appliances—restoring your home to sparkling condition while working around your furniture and belongings.

**What's Included:**
- Everything in Regular Cleaning, plus:
- Baseboards and door frames
- Ceiling fans and light fixtures
- Window sills and blinds
- Behind and under appliances (where accessible)
- Interior cabinet faces
- Detailed grout and tile work

**Best For:** Occupied homes with furniture, seasonal deep cleans, preparing for guests

### Service 3: Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning

**Description:**
Intensive cleaning for empty properties before you move in or after you move out. Every corner, cabinet, and closet is scrubbed and sanitized, ensuring the home is move-in ready or returned to pristine condition.

**What's Included:**
- All surfaces in every room (no furniture blocking)
- Inside all cabinets, closets, and drawers
- All appliances (interior and exterior)
- Windows, sills, and tracks
- Walls, baseboards, and doors
- Bathrooms completely sanitized
- Floors scrubbed and detailed

**Best For:** Empty properties, moving day, landlords, real estate agents
